Discrete Event Simulation

Discrete Time?

  • A list of events that occur at a certain time
  • Nothing is continuous, everything needs to be scheduled.
  • Allows for greater deal of concurrency
  • Skips to the next event

    • Purpose?

  • Simulate the power consumption of appliances in a house
  • Find a suitable simulation environment.
  • Get a real world example to simulate.
  • A timeline of events of appliance use
  • A simulation fitted around real data

    • Overall Objective?

  • To build a platform where we can simulate the effect of domestic energy trading
  • See how it may be possible to have areas generate power
  • See how it may affect the energy market if homes started selling energy to nearby businesses and factories
